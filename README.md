## 🚀 About Me

I'm a **Senior Backend Software Engineer** with 4+ years of experience designing and shipping scalable Java & Spring Boot microservices, event-driven systems with Kafka, and cloud-native platforms on AWS. I love building reliable, observable, well-tested systems — and sharing what I learn along the way.

When I'm not writing code, I'm creating content on **YouTube**, writing on **Medium**, and sharing daily life on **Instagram**.

- 🔭 Currently building canary health-check platforms & streaming data pipelines
- 🌱 Always learning — currently exploring Go, advanced system design, and AI-assisted development
- 🎥 Creating developer content on YouTube & writing tech articles on Medium
- ⚡ Fun fact: I automate everything I do twice — even my coffee orders ☕
- 🤝 Open to collaborating on backend, cloud, and developer-tooling projects
